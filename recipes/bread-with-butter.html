Tomago Kakke Gohan

Ingredients:

  • loaf of your favorite bread (such as a crusty baguette or a hearty whole wheat loaf)
  • Butter, preferably at room temperature

Instructions:

    Slice the bread into desired thickness. You can go for thicker slices for a heartier bite or thinner slices for a lighter snack. Spread a generous amount of butter onto each slice of bread. If the butter is too cold to spread easily, you can let it sit at room temperature for a few minutes or gently microwave it for a few seconds until softened. Serve and enjoy! Optionally, you can toast the bread slices before spreading the butter for a warm and crispy treat.

That's it! FA simple yet satisfying snack or side dish featuring just two ingredients: bread and butter. Perfect for any time of the day!
