Odin Recipes

Ingredients:

  • all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 250ml milk
  • 30g unsalted butter, melted
  • Additional butter or oil for greasing the pan
  • Optional toppings: maple syrup, fresh fruits, whipped cream, chocolate chips, etc.

Instructions:

  1. Place the all-purpose flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt into the mixing bowl of the Thermomix.
  2. Close the lid and mix the dry ingredients together for a few seconds on speed 3 until well combined.
  3. Add the egg, milk, and melted butter to the mixing bowl.
  4. Close the lid and mix the batter on speed 4 for about 10-15 seconds, or until the batter is smooth and well combined. Scrape down the sides of the bowl if needed and mix again briefly.
  5. Let the batter rest for a few minutes while you preheat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  6. Once the skillet is hot, lightly grease it with butter or oil.
  7. Pour about 1/4 cup of the pancake batter onto the skillet for each pancake, spreading it slightly with the back of a spoon if necessary to form a round shape.
  8. Cook the pancakes for 2-3 minutes on one side, or until bubbles start to form on the surface and the edges look set.
  9. Flip the pancakes using a spatula and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes on the other side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  10. Transfer the cooked pancakes to a plate and repeat with the remaining batter, greasing the skillet as needed.
  11. Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings, such as maple syrup, fresh fruits, whipped cream, or chocolate chips.
  12. Enjoy your delicious homemade pancakes made with the Thermomix!

Note: Feel free to customize the recipe by adding flavorings such as vanilla extract or cinnamon to the batter, or by incorporating mix-ins like blueberries or chocolate chips. You can also adjust the thickness of the batter by adding more milk if desired.
