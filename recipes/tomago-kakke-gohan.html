Tomago Kakke Gohan

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of cooked Japanese rice
  • 1 fresh egg
  • Soy sauce (optional)
  • Green onions, thinly sliced (optional)
  • (seaweed) strips for garnish (optional)
  • Furikake (Japanese rice seasoning) for extra flavor (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook Japanese rice according to package instructions or using a rice cooker until it's fluffy and slightly sticky.
  2. While the rice is still hot, crack a fresh egg into a small bowl.
  3. Pour the raw egg over the hot rice.
  4. Using chopsticks or a spoon, quickly and gently mix the egg into the rice until it's evenly coated.
  5. Add a splash of soy sauce to taste, if desired.
  6. Garnish with thinly sliced green onions, nori strips, or furikake for added flavor and texture.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your simple and delicious tamago kake gohan!

Note: Make sure to use fresh, high-quality eggs for this recipe, as the egg will be consumed raw. If you're concerned about food safety, you can use pasteurized eggs. Additionally, feel free to adjust the seasoning and garnishes according to your preference.
